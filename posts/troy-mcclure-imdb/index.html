Troy McClure

Troy McClure is a suave Hollywood leading man, whose breakout roles in the 1970s catapaulted him to worldwide superstar status. While both an accomplished film and TV star, McClure regularly shows his range by with appearances in infomercials and self-help videos. His slick, yet sincere performances are matched only by his classic Hollywood looks.
